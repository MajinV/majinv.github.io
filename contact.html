How it works

Consultation

I provide a free initial consultation for your event. At this meeting your requirements will be discussed in detail to establish your personal style choices. If you have ideas for colours, a theme or suggestions for the style and look that you would like to achieve, this is a great opportunity to explore and discuss these.

We can have the initial meeting in person, using Skype, or on the phone if you prefer. There will be lots of visual aspects to explore, so in person is best where possible!

Estimate

You will receive an estimate for your wedding decorations within one week of the consultation. I will also provide a description of elements discussed and to be used within the displays.

Prices vary depending on several factors, but this will be discussed at the consultation along with your budget. I will endeavour to work to your budget and find a way to achieve the look you want at a price that suits you.

Your dream wedding!

Every element made from polymer clay will be an individual work of art, hand crafted with love just for you. To compliment the exquisite clay elements, only the highest quality of product will be used to enhance the decorations for your wedding. My designs will reflect everything we’ve discussed in the consultation.

Finally, as nothing is perishable the items can be kept or gifted as the perfect memento from your special day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose my wedding centrepieces?

This can be quite daunting but once you get going you will quickly find that you know what you like and, just as importantly, what you don’t like.

The best thing to do is simply start gathering images of any styles, items or arrangements that catch your eye. You don’t have to love them – but if they catch your eye, there must be a reason why. Pinterest is a great source of inspiration.

In the consultation, we’ll explore all the images you’ve saved and discuss each one. We’ll work out what’s important to you, establish whether there’s a theme running through your choices and start designing something beautiful for you. The images you save will be a springboard for the consultation!

What happens in a consultation?

Based on your location we can arrange a meeting place, if local to the area I can travel to your home for the consultation or we can meet somewhere else if you would prefer. The consultation can take up to a couple of hours.

I will go through images with you, discussing what you like and write notes on your wishes. This allows me to get to know you, understand your style, the things you like and just as importantly, the things you don’t like. There is no right or wrong, it is your wedding and I am simply there to assist you in creating a beautiful, visual narrative to your perfect day.

Be as open as you can be with me as it will help me to truly understand you and your requirements as a couple. Don’t be afraid to say when you “love” or “dislike” something!

Finally, it is really useful for you to have a rough idea of your budget, this is an important element in ensuring we get the brief right for you. By the end of the consultation, we’ll have put a design together for you that fits your budget and style.

When will we receive an estimate?

Following the consultation, I will prepare your estimate and send this within 1 week of the consultation (this may take slightly longer during peak wedding season, but you will be advised if this is the case at the consultation). You may want to make some changes which I will amend and resend as appropriate.

What if we want to make changes to our order?

It is not uncommon for your order to change slightly. However, please note that as all the items are handmade especially for you, which is a time sensitive process, there will be a cut-off point in which changes can no longer be made. This will vary from wedding to wedding. Larger scale weddings will need to finalize sooner than smaller weddings. This will be advised to you at the same time as your initial estimate is sent through, so you are fully informed.

How much do wedding centrepieces and table décor cost?

There is no one set price and the price will vary from wedding to wedding. I work to a variety of budgets and I believe that everyone should be able to get the look they want no matter what their budget.

If your budget is on the smaller side I have many techniques to help keep the costs down for you but this will obviously require compromise on some elements, such as how many handmade clay flowers you have. As every element is created on a bespoke basis I will only be able to work out a price for you once I’ve spoken to you in depth about your requirements.

What are we ‘supposed’ to have for our wedding decoration? What do other people have?

I can’t stress enough that there are no rules! It’s your wedding and you should have whatever you want. However if you do need a bit of inspiration here is a list of things frequently ordered:

  • Table centrepieces
  • Top table display
  • Table confetti
  • Wedding Favours
  • Ceremony decorations
  • Pew ends
  • Hair decorations
  • Button Holes (usually for the Groom, Best Man, Ushers, Fathers of the couple and any other close male friends or relatives)
  • Corsages (like feminine button holes) for the mothers of the couple and any other important female friends or relatives

At the moment I don’t do bouquets however if this is of interest to you please do let me know as it is something I could quite easily look into for you.

What area do you venue dress?

If your wish is for me to set up the venue for you on your wedding day then the venue area that I cover is as per the below (if you are just outside one of the areas please do contact me as this is a rough guide only)

  • East London
  • Out as far as Colchester
  • Out as far as Cambridge
  • Out as far as Dunstable

Alternatively as the items are non-perishable I can look at sending these to most locations within the UK although there will be a delivery cost which you will be responsible for and you will have to arrange set up on the day.

Do you always visit the wedding venue with every client?

Not always, no. If you’re getting married in a venue I know well or your requirements are quite straight forward there’s no need. If it is a wedding venue I’m not so familiar with, or if you require particular or unusual arrangements, I am happy to come along and meet you there to visualise your big day and see what we can do for you.

What happens on our wedding day?

On your wedding day, I will set up a few hours before your ceremony. If you have ordered button holes, corsages or hair accessories I will likely arrange for these to be delivered to you in advance of the day or on the day whichever ends up suiting the best. I am happy to liaise with your venue if necessary and fit in around times. I can be flexible on this and will work around times to suit you. Please note that I am unable to do take down of the items.

What if it’s snowing and you can’t get to us on the day?

I will endeavour to meet my commitments and do everything in my power to overcome any problems. My aim is to always set up the wedding to make the day as stress free for you as possible. However, as the products are non-perishable, should the weather forecast indicate a potential problem we can always arrange to deliver them to you in advance for you to make your own set up arrangements with the venue. Although not ideal, we will have ensured your day continues to be a success.

How and when do we pay to secure the order?

A 40% deposit is required to book Eternal Posy. This is payable as soon as possible and we also ask that you both sign and complete an Agreement Form to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due 6 weeks prior to the wedding. Payments can be made by online transfer.

This information will be outlined in our contract that will provided when you decide to order.
